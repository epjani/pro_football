MICHAEL STRAHAN

Strahan, who was also noted as one of the best defensive
ends defending against the run, enjoyed his finest season in
2001 when he established the new NFL record with 22.5
sacks and led the league with a career-high seven forced
fumbles. For his efforts he was named the NFL's Defensive
Player of the Year. Two seasons later, he added a second
NFL sack crown when he registered 18.5 quarterback
takedowns.
